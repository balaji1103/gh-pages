Pick a plan

Dibya Published on December 4, 2020

 

Mother’s Care

  • Nutrition & Wellness Care during Preconception
  • Pregnancy Diet & Wellness Care (Trimester Wise)
  • Diet & Wellness care during Lactation Period
  • Post Natal Holistic Wellness including Diet for Child
  • Rehabilitation including coming back to shape & structure

General

Today being pregnant is mostly by ‘choice’ than ‘chance’ but the essence remains univocal i.e. of ‘creation’; where a woman has been courageous & bold enough to undergo the most painful journey carved out for minimum of three years.

The process starts with generation of this idea or ‘choice’ of a to be mother to conceive……. look after trimester wise (03 months each), Being in 6-9 months the most critical…. Post-natal care of mother and child….and Rehabilitation including coming back to own shape & size.

Modus Operandi

A correct diet counselling is offered by our dietician & nutrition team prior to preparing yourself for conceiving the child, during nine months of pregnancy and post-delivery again regaining the health, shape and structure without restricting much on food choices of the mother. Idea is to modulate the food and supplement to the ‘to be mother’, ‘pregnant mother’ and ‘the mother’.

A very scientific approach to handle the dietary & wellness requirement is applied to tackle individual requirements as per medical condition of the mother. A healthy pregnancy time and post pregnancy rehabilitation is taken care off by qualified and experienced Dietician, Nutritionist & Wellness Therapist.

Programme Benefits & Problems Addressed

  • How much weight should I gain? How much extra calorie do I need…. (What if I am having twins as expecting)?
  • Which all essential supplements I should have to nourish myself & my baby well apart from routine medication (Am I overdoing it?).
  • Which common food I should avoid as per my medical reports, weight & lifestyle?
  • How to avoid my food cravings, morning sickness & heartburns?
  • How much physical exercise is ok for me and on which exercises I should dwell upon during & after pregnancy?
  • Till which stage I should be working at my office/ home etc.?
  • Post the birth of the baby; How to plan the initial 6 months for his/ her food requirements?
  • How to reclaim my shape & size again without getting into vicious cycle of fad diet plans and still catering for the healthy requirements as a nascent mother?

Child Nutrition

  • During Pregnancy
  • Children’s Food Allergy & Misconceptions
  • Nutrition Plan for Children to cope up the Peer and Education & Environmental stress in Growing age.

General

While requisite nutrition is vital throughout childhood, it is in particular, very important & crucial during the first five years of child life because that is the time when children grow rapidly and entirely dependent on their parents for their food habits & intake. These are the foundation years for their different health requirements during their growth, mind development & physical activities. It’s also important to understand what the children should eat, the amount, the frequency, their food allergies, their fussy habits, tiffin box intolerances, snacking habits, to make a base for their overall development as toddlers and further as adolescents.

Modus Operandi

 Infant Nutrition

  • Imbibing healthy Mother Infant Diet
  • Initial 6 months advice
  • Nutritional behaviour kickstart during 1st year
  • Immunity boosting during early days through mother-child co-opted
  • Advice on keeping child away from mobile/TV while eating
  • Advice on Processed/junk food including snacking and inheritance of good diet in initial year

Child Nutrition

  • Focus on child’s need in growing years
  • Food allergies and advices
  • Imbibing home food habits with minor deviations in daily life.
  • The tiffin box advices
  • Special advices to child nutrition with special conditions like Diabetes, Obesity, Growth issues.
  • Correct gap food habits
  • Resolving better Snacking habits
  • Encouragement to outdoor activities rather Indoor games.
  • Child Psychological counselling, stress etc.

Shaping The Beautiful You (Postpartum Diet Plan)

General

The after effects of pregnancy & child birth tends to be with you for a while and these stubborn post pregnancy weight refuses to go away. Your body is still overcoming a huge change in terms of sleep deprivation, irritation in terms of baby blues, your body shape & structure. So, find out where your body is at? Your pregnancy has been challenging and now it compounds more with the baby care, family–work balance, your personal supplemental needs and a kick start nourishment for coming back to your own size. Discover a planned post–partum weight loss with a safe customized healthful diet, exercise and yoga. Experience the same beautiful you donning the same wardrobe within a year of this post -partum change in your life.

 Modus Operandi

  • 6 Months Shape up Regime to regain Shape, Weight & structure post pregnancy without damaging the gut and body internal.
  • Healthy advice for Mother and Lap child.
  • Moderate exercises and yoga advice.
  • Immunity building and care for mother and child.
  • Special care advice to cesarean operated mothers.
  • Consultation for working mothers: Diet & routine healthcare
