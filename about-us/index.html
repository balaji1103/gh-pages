About Us

Dibya Published on December 4, 2020

MetamorphWithDibya is a complete Wellness platform managed by Dietician & Wellnesspreneur Dibya Prakash & her team. It is a complete holistic wellness platform mainly focused on ‘To be Mother’, ‘The Pregnant Mother’, ‘The Mother’ and her child wellness programmes for diet, wellness, customized exercises & Yoga involvement aimed at personalized plans & prescription with hands on guidance for the beautiful moms and her child.

 

Mrs. Dibya Prakash is a Wellness Therapist, Dietician (Life Member Of IDA), Blogger and Certified Trainer. Her qualifications include MBA from Symbiosis Institute of Business Management Pune, Masters in Dietetics & Food Service Management, Diploma in Nutrition, Diploma in Child Psychology, Bachelor of Pharmacy & Professional Diploma in Weight loss and Fitness. Dibya combines the evidence based holistic practices, proven nutritional treatments with suave yoga mix and exercises for individualistic tailor-made requirements. Dibya’s strength is in utilizing food as a medicine, but also acknowledge the mind body connection, she is certified & acquired acumen of understanding modern psychology of to be moms, pregnant moms and nutritional care of both mother & child.

As a working mother she understands the requirement of proper guidance before, during and after pregnancy, when your near and dear ones are not there to guide you at every step. Also, she understands the life challenges faced by today’s working & young ladies who have to stay away from family and feel stressed throughout this beautiful journey of life.

For 10 years now, Dibya is working with women to make this journey as the best memory of their life by improving their nutritional intake, their physical fitness through customized yoga and exercise mix, their mental well being by lending patient ear and counselling for weight loss and conceiving, improving pre and post-natal health of mother and child, working with mother and children for better awareness and creating an environment where child develops good food habits without much restrictions and disruptions. She has helped ladies to rediscover their beautiful side again after pregnancy without disrupting their own health in long term and giving best to the new born.

She believes in ethical diet practices, rather working for short term, she believes in educating ladies for importance of adapting healthy life style choices throughout their life without much restrictions. She understands individual strength and flexibility before prescribing yoga & exercise for physical fitness.

Dibya & her team is genuinely personable and warmly well spoken with boundless empathy and good listening skills that help her to extract the right information from you to tailor your treatment plan.

  1. Mother’s Care
  • Nutrition & Wellness Care during Preconception
  • Pregnancy Diet & Wellness Care (Trimester Wise)
  • Diet & wellness care during Lactation Period
  • Post Natal Holistic Wellness including Diet for Child
  • Rehabilitation including coming back to shape & structure
  1. Child Nutrition
  • During Pregnancy
  • Children’s Food Allergy & Misconceptions
  • Nutrition Plan for Children to cope up the Peer Pressure, Education & Environmental stress in Growing age.
  1. Beautiful You

Specially designed programmes for the Beautiful Ladies to be in correct post-partum weight & shape.    (Only for those who are ready to wait for the result rather shortcuts)
